Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical skills to use BotGenie?
+

Not at all! BotGenie is designed for everyone. If you can send a text message, you can set up your AI bot. Our step-by-step guide walks you through everything.

What is Kimi K2.5 and why do you use it?
+

Kimi K2.5 is a powerful AI model that understands conversations naturally. We chose it because it's excellent at understanding context and speaking multiple languages — perfect for real customer conversations.

Can my bot handle multiple languages?
+

Yes! Your bot can automatically detect and respond in the language your customer uses, including English, Zulu, Swahili, French, Portuguese, and many more.

When will WhatsApp be available?
+

WhatsApp integration is coming soon! Join our waitlist and we'll notify you the moment it's ready. In the meantime, you can get started with Telegram right away.

Can I try it before paying?
+

Absolutely! Our Starter plan is completely free. You get 500 messages per month at no cost. Upgrade only when your business needs more.

What happens when a customer asks something the bot doesn't know?
+

Your bot will gracefully let the customer know and can notify you or your team so a human can step in. You're always in control of the conversation.
